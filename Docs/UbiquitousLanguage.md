# Ubiquitous Language

## ATM Card

A physical card issued by the bank and used to authenticate a customer.

---

## Customer

The owner of one or more accounts.

---

## PIN

Personal Identification Number associated with a card.

---

## Account

A financial account belonging to a customer.

Types:

- Savings
- Checking

---

## Withdrawal

Removal of funds from an account through an ATM.

---

## Transfer

Movement of funds between two accounts.

---

## Daily Withdrawal Limit

Maximum amount that can be withdrawn during a single day.

---

## Confiscated Card

A card retained by the ATM due to:

- Lost card
- Stolen card
- Three failed PIN attempts
